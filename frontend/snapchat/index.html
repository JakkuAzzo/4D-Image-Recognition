How it works

  • We compute FaceNet-like pointer vectors for faces in public stories visible to the logged-in user.
  • Pointers are stored with hashed usernames, date/time, and approximate location in our ledger.
  • Every 30 minutes a scheduled task updates recent regions (rate-limited; regions with legal restrictions are skipped).
  • When you upload photos, we compare your model against this pointer set and show potential matches for your review.
